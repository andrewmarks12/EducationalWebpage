The History of Lacrosse!

The history of lacrosse dates back hundreds of years to the Native American tribes.

How was the sport created?

The Native American people played lacrosse to please "The Creator" a godlike figure that created the game of lacrosse as a form of medicine and problem solver for the people. The Natives used lacrosse to stay in shape, heal, and fight battles. The game of lacrosse was played on field that were miles long by miles wide and the games lasted for days. The games were a way to settle disputes in the tribes.

The positions played in lacrosse are listed below:

  • Midfield (Short Stick, Long Stick, & Faceoff)
  • Defense
  • Attack
  • Goalie
